## What is Matcha Green Tea Powder?

**Matcha green tea powder** is a finely ground powder made from specially grown green tea leaves. These leaves are carefully cultivated in shade conditions to increase chlorophyll and nutrient levels.

Unlike regular tea, where leaves are steeped and removed, matcha is consumed in whole form. This means you get **100% of the nutrients**, making it far more powerful than standard green tea.
